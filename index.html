What do I do?

I like to make cool things.

I develop applications for the web. I know my way around Django and Flask, most of the time.

Sometimes I make games. I have a few years of experience with XNA in particular, and I've also dabbled at times with Flixel, Irrlicht, and Pygame.

In general, I think I'm pretty good at C# and Python.

In the recent past I've worked on (or am still working on):

  • Wiski (Python, Flask), a lightweight Markdown-rendered wiki
  • eyBlog (Python, Django), a simple blog
  • Quimbu (Python, Flask), a link-sharing bulletin board
  • Pebble2048 (C, PebbleSDK), an adaptation of 2048 for the Pebble smartwatch, currently the second most popular 2048 app on the Pebble app store.
