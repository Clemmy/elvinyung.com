What do I do?

I like to develop applications for the web. I know my way around Django and Flask, most of the time.

Sometimes I make games. I have a few years of experience with XNA in particular, and I've also dabbled at times with Flixel, Irrlicht, and Pygame.

In the recent past I've worked on (or am still working on):

  • Wiskid, a lightweight Markdown-rendered wiki written in Flask
  • eyBlogd, a simple Django blog
  • Quimbud, a Flask link-sharing bulletin boar
  • Pebble2048, an adaptation of 2048 for the Pebble smartwatch, currently the second most popular 2048 app on the Pebble app store.
